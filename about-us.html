About Us

Welcome to our Typing Test Platform!

This platform was created by a group of classmates as part of a project to help users improve their typing speed and accuracy through fun and interactive tests.

Our goals:

  • Help users practice typing with real-time feedback
  • Allow users to view their progress over time
  • Provide a simple and user-friendly interface

Whether you're preparing for a typing exam or just want to type faster, our tool is here to help!
